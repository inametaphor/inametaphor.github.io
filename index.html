About me

I started coding in the '90s on Geocities and Angelfire, when personal websites were still an uncharted frontier and we all used iframes and client-side includes and tables to format our websites. I then took a 20-ish year break, missing the transition to HTML5, CSS growing up, and ES6 for JavaScript entirely. The world of web design changed drastically in 20 years, and I'm just trying to keep up. Don't be surprised if my dislike of stark black and white make some of the ol' Geocities color keep popping up...but I'll try to keep the tiling background images to a minimum.
 
I neither work in tech (directly; I'm in a tech-adjacent field), nor want to work in tech. All the projects on this page are for passion, learning, or because I wanted it to exist enough that I decided to make it myself.
